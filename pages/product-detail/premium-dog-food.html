Product Description

Our Premium Grain-Free Dog Food is crafted with the highest quality ingredients to provide complete and balanced nutrition for your canine companion. Made with real meat as the first ingredient, this formula supports lean muscle development and provides sustained energy throughout the day.

Key Benefits:

  • Grain-Free Formula: Ideal for dogs with grain sensitivities or allergies.
  • Real Meat First: High-quality protein source as the primary ingredient.
  • Digestive Support: Contains prebiotics and probiotics for optimal gut health.
  • Skin & Coat Health: Rich in Omega-3 and Omega-6 fatty acids for a shiny coat and healthy skin.
  • Joint Support: Added glucosamine and chondroitin for mobility and joint health.
  • No Artificial Ingredients: Free from artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives.

Ingredients:

Deboned Chicken, Chicken Meal, Turkey Meal, Sweet Potatoes, Peas, Potatoes, Chicken Fat (preserved with mixed tocopherols), Dried Ground Potatoes, Tomato Pomace, Natural Chicken Flavor, Salmon Oil, Ground Flaxseed, Blueberries, Cranberries, Apples, Carrots, Spinach, Pumpkin, Vitamins (Vitamin E Supplement, Niacin, d-Calcium Pantothenate, Vitamin A Supplement, Riboflavin Supplement, Thiamine Mononitrate, Vitamin D3 Supplement, Pyridoxine Hydrochloride, Folic Acid, Biotin, Vitamin B12 Supplement), Minerals (Zinc Proteinate, Iron Proteinate, Copper Proteinate, Manganese Proteinate, Sodium Selenite, Calcium Iodate), Choline Chloride, Dried Chicory Root, Glucosamine Hydrochloride, Chondroitin Sulfate, Yucca Schidigera Extract, Dried Lactobacillus plantarum Fermentation Product, Dried Lactobacillus casei Fermentation Product, Dried Enterococcus faecium Fermentation Product, Dried Lactobacillus acidophilus Fermentation Product, Rosemary Extract.
